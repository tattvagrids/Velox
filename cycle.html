Premium Maintenance Guide

Protect your investment. Proper care ensures your Velox performs at peak efficiency for decades.

Carbon Frame Care

  • Cleaning: Use only mild soapy water and a microfiber cloth. Never use high-pressure washers, as water can force its way into bearings.
  • Inspection: Check for deep scratches or delamination after any crash. Carbon is incredibly strong but requires professional assessment if compromised.

Electronic Drivetrain (Di2/eTap)

  • Battery: Charge your derailleur batteries once a month or every 1000km. Do not let them drain to 0% for prolonged periods.
  • Lubrication: Apply premium dry or wet lube (depending on weather) to the chain every 200km. Wipe off excess to prevent grime buildup.

Hydraulic Disc Brakes

  • Bedding In: New rotors must be 'bed-in' by performing 10-15 controlled stops from 25km/h to walking pace without fully locking the wheels.
  • Bleeding: Brakes should be bled by a certified Velox technician every 12-18 months to ensure absolute stopping power.
